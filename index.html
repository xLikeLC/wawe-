ABOUT THE SCHOOL,SURFING,CANARIES

The Canary Islands are often called the European Hawaii for a reason – it's an ideal place for surfing enthusiasts: stable warm winds year-round, sandy beaches, kilometers of untouched expanses, and crystal-clear waters. The real mecca for surfers becomes Fuerteventura – the second-largest island in the archipelago.

This is the best place in Spain for learning to surf. Besides the fact that huge Atlantic waves come here in winter and autumn, Fuerteventura boasts numerous shallow calm lagoons, perfect for training beginner surfers.

Surfing in the Canary Islands is available year-round – both waves and suitable weather are always present. Moreover, both professionals and beginners can find waves suitable for their skill level. It is considered that this place is the best for learning to surf, making it possible to smoothly progress to a new level without difficulties.

Listing the advantages, it's worth mentioning that Fuerteventura has the longest beaches in the Canaries, making it impossible to imagine them crowded with tourists. You will only encounter those who, like you, share one goal – learning to surf.

Four hours of exciting activities a day will help you conquer the waves with confidence and feel like a real surfer.

Whether you are just curious about what surfing is or have already experienced the thrill of riding the waves but want to improve your skills, our surfing school can offer training for any skill level. Four hours of exciting lessons per day will help you confidently conquer the waves and feel like a true surfer.

The main advantages of our Russian school are high-quality teaching and organization of classes, safety (all our clients are insured by Allianz), excellent equipment (provided for the entire course, including soft, safe boards for beginners).

First, we assess the level of riding and physical fitness, and only then do we develop a training program. We can assure you that after completing the training, you will not only gain a lot of fantastic experiences but will also elevate your skills to a new level.

A huge advantage of our school is its own teaching methodology. We have developed our own style of teaching surfing, which allows beginners to get on the board as quickly as possible.

It was our methodology and our teachers that allowed us to pass the certification of the Surfing Federation Canary Islands - FEDERACIÓN CANARIA DE SURF. This means that our school meets all world standards. Recertification is carried out annually and this allows us to always strive for improving learning.

CONQUER THE PEAKS

Our surf school teachers are among the best surfers in the Canary Islands. Among them is the European longboard champion, who will happily share his experience with both beginners and more experienced surfers. All members of the teaching staff are certified members of the Canary Islands Surfing Federation. Some of them know Russian, which definitely positions us as a Russian surf school abroad!

The entire coaching staff regularly participates in seminars and advanced training courses held around the world by various well-known surf clubs and federations. Therefore, in our surf school you can rest assured: you are taught by the best coaches!

The surfing teaching method is designed for both beginners, intermediate-level surfers, and TOP-level surfers. The coaches find an approach to everyone, despite the fact that surfing lessons at our school are taught in groups. However, we offer personal surf lessons - just you and a coach!
